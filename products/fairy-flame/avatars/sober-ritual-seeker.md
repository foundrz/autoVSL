# Kindergarten Simple Pitch — Sober Ritual-Seeker 🥉 (LHF #3)

**AVATAR & AGE:** Person who quit (or is quitting) drinking and needs a new evening ritual, 28–55

## THE PROBLEM (80/20 Most Important)
**Primary Problems:**
- "I can't relax without a drink — alcohol was the only thing that let me breathe."
- "I can't get through the 6pm hour — the ritual is what I miss, not the taste."
- "I can't talk to people without the edge coming off first."
- "My anxiety is back full-volume now that the alcohol is gone."

**Secondary Problems:**
- NA beer feels like pretending; tea does nothing
- Scared of trading one crutch for another
- White-knuckling evenings, waiting for the urge to pass

## THE EMOTIONAL PAIN
"I've had crippling anxiety my whole life, and the first time I caught a buzz it
was like I could breathe for the first time. I quit because it was killing me —
but nobody gave me back the breathing part. I'm proud of my sobriety and
completely exhausted by it. What do I put in the glass now?"

## THE ROOT CAUSE OF THE PROBLEM
They believe the drinking was never the point — it was self-medication for
anxiety plus a ritual that marked the end of the day. Remove the alcohol and
both jobs are vacant. Willpower fills neither.

## THE ROOT SOLUTION (Big Idea)
**The "Ah Ha":** "Sometimes we just need to replace the liquid in the glass but
maintain our ritual." A single calm-without-poison gummy can BE the new 6pm
ritual — the exhale without the hangover, the shame, or the slide backward.

## HOW THE PRODUCT SOLVES THIS
1. One gummy becomes the new end-of-day marker — same ritual slot, new contents
2. The calm arrives without a buzz — present, not buzzed, not numb
3. Over weeks, the baseline anxiety that drove the drinking gets quieter on its own

**Key Differentiator:** Not another thing that owns you — no hangover, no
"just one more," lab report in every pouch so you know exactly what you're
putting in your body (which matters a lot to someone protecting their sobriety).

## THE AMAZING BENEFITS (80/20 Most Important)
**Primary Benefits:**
- BEFORE: white-knuckling the evening → AFTER: actually looking forward to the ritual again
- BEFORE: anxiety at full volume since quitting → AFTER: "helped my anxiety without making me feel sedated or disconnected"
- BEFORE: waking up foggy and ashamed → AFTER: clear mornings ("I notice the difference every morning")

**Secondary Benefits:**
- People who microdose report drinking less or losing interest in alcohol
- The money math sells itself vs. a bar tab

## THE EMOTIONAL BENEFITS
"I'm not pretending anymore and I'm not suffering through it either. I found my
exhale. I get to keep my sobriety AND my peace — I didn't know I could have
both."

## SIDE EFFECTS
- Most Common: gentle, gradual; no buzz — which is exactly the point
- 80/20 Takeaway: nothing about it behaves like the thing they quit

---
**Sophistication:** Level 2 (problem aware — "what do I replace it with?" is
their literal question). Meet them at the ritual, introduce the mechanism.
**Market size:** 7/10 — r/stopdrinking is massive and the "replace the ritual"
language recurs across dozens of high-scoring comments; sober-curious trend
expands it.
**Best spokesperson:** someone with real sober time who found their new ritual;
brother-to-brother / sister-to-sister register.
