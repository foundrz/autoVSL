# Founder Story — TRUE story (Colton, founder of liitt)

> Captured 2026-07-07 at product intake, verbatim. This is the founder's actual
> true story. Core phrase: "feel like yourself again."

I'm the founder of liitt, where we help people explore the benefits of
microdosing—especially those struggling with depression, anxiety, or PTSD.

Now, whenever people hear that, the first question I usually get is:
"Wait… how are you even able to do this? Aren't there laws against mushrooms
that grow straight from the ground?"

The truth is, there's a workaround for everything. But before I explain why,
let me share my story.

Years ago, I was married to the woman I loved, and we were expecting our first
child. On the night before our first anniversary, as we were driving to
celebrate, I fell asleep behind the wheel.

When I woke up in the hospital, the doctors told me something I'll never forget:
"We only brought back one body from the accident."

That moment shattered me. Afterward, I tried everything to get my life back on
track—therapy, medication, even things like yoga and breathwork. But nothing
worked. Nothing could pull my mind out of the darkness.

It wasn't until I discovered psychedelics that things began to change.
They gave me a sense of clarity, of possibility, and for the first time—I felt
like myself again.

During the darkest times, I made myself a promise:
"If I ever find my way out of this, I'm going to share it with others—because
no one should have to suffer like this."

And that's why I founded liitt. To make safe, guided access to natural
solutions available for people who are dealing with depression, anxiety, PTSD,
brain fog, or simply the need to restore their creativity and mental clarity.

So if you've tried everything else and you're still searching for relief,
I invite you to try microdosing with us. Click the link below, place your
order, and you'll receive it just like every one of our other customers.

Give it a try—and see how it can help you finally feel like yourself again.
