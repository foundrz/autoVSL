# Master Shot List — Fairy Flame × Burned-Out 40s Mom (Scripts 01–10)

One shoot plan covers all 10 scripts. Three talent setups: **Mom (48F)**,
**Nurse (52F)**, **Founder (38M)**. Each clip 3–5 seconds. Shots marked (AI)
are good candidates for AI generation; the rest favor real footage.

## ✅ WHAT YOU NEED

**Props:**
- Fairy Flame pouch + loose gummies (hero product)
- Printed lab report / COA sheet (major trust prop — used in 8 of 10 scripts)
- Old printed photo of Mom talent "before kids" (bookend prop for 01 & 06)
- Smartphone (camera roll scroll, 3am screen glow)
- Wine glass + bottle · coffee mug + second cup · energy drink can
- Supplement drawer/bottles (the failed pile)
- Walking shoes by the door
- Generic unbranded mushroom-gummy pouch (gas-station stand-in for 10)
- Kitchen counter with crumbs · couch · bed · bathroom mirror
- Calendar or phone day-counter (Day 7 / 21 / 45)
- Delivery box on doorstep

**Wardrobe:**
- Mom: real mom clothes — before = worn/rumpled layers; after = same style, put-together, brighter color
- Nurse: scrubs OR smart-casual + lanyard (test both credibility reads)
- Founder: plain dark tee/flannel, no logos — quiet, sincere

**Tips:**
- Each clip 3–5 seconds · natural light · tripod or propped phone
- Film BEFORE and AFTER versions of the same scenario (same framing = the contrast sells)
- Real emotion over perfect performance; A24 warm-intimate, never stock-footage energy
- No neon, no fast cuts, no fake smiles, no before/after split screens (brand negative prompt)

## 🎣 HOOK SHOTS (14)

**Identity/Photo (01, 06):**
- Hands open a closet box, pull out old photo (pause on it)
- Camera-roll thumb-scroll, stops on a 10-year-old photo
- Mom holding photo next to her face in mirror
- Silent tear while looking at photo (restrained, real)

**Contrarian objects (05, 09):**
- Wine glass mid-pour, freeze/look to camera
- Coffee mug pushed away across counter
- Second-cup pour with visible regret
- Energy drink crack, then set down

**Nurse authority (02, 07, 08):**
- Nurse straight to camera, warm-serious open
- "45–55" title-card moment (talent gestures to side for insert)

**Founder (04, 10):**
- Founder close-up, quiet beat before speaking (rock-bottom line)
- Founder flips generic gummy pouch to its blank/mystery label
- Blurry smoke-shop shelf pan
- Doorstep delivery box pickup

## 😫 PROBLEM SHOTS (18)

**The fog & exhaustion:**
- 10am desk fog stare (slept 8 hours, still gone)
- Forehead on kitchen counter, deep breath
- 2pm wall: slow slump at desk
- Nap that fixes nothing — waking still tired
- Mail/paper pile on table ("can't get in gear")

**The snapping & guilt:**
- Snap at kids over crumbs → instant regret face
- Hand to mouth after raising voice
- Kids go quiet in background

**The 3am:**
- Eyes open in dark, ceiling stare
- Phone screen glow 3:07 AM
- Hand on chest, heart pounding
- Partner asleep, she's not

**Failed solutions:**
- Supplement drawer opens — bottles rattle (half-used)
- Walking shoes by the door, untouched
- Flat pill-stare into middle distance (numbed, not better)
- Therapy-office waiting room energy
- "Take more walks" prescription-pad insert (tasteful)
- Fun things abandoned: garden gloves / book / craft bin gathering dust

## 🗣️ SPOKESPERSON SHOTS (per talent — 8 angles each)

Straight to camera (main) · 15–20° left · 15–20° right · seated kitchen table ·
standing counter lean · walking-and-talking hallway · close-up intense (hooks,
rock-bottom lines) · hands-visible product demo

**Tone passes to capture (each talent):**
- Mom: confessional (problems) · bright/relieved (transformation) · girlfriend-to-girlfriend (CTA)
- Nurse: serious-direct (hooks) · empathetic (validation) · reassuring-expert (mechanism) · warm (results)
- Founder: quiet-broken (origin) · resolute (promise/mission) · plain-spoken trust (transparency)

## ⭐ BENEFIT SHOTS (16)

**Herself again:**
- Singing/humming while cooking (THE money shot — scripts 01, 03)
- Laughing at husband's dumb joke on couch
- Dumb joke in grocery store, stranger smiles
- Mirror glance: flat stare GONE, small real smile
- Photo held beside present-day face — she matches it again (match cut)

**Family gets her back:**
- Kids pile into her lap, she's present
- Daughter hug — "you seem lighter" beat
- Patient homework help, zero edge
- Family dinner where she's IN it, not serving it

**Calm & clarity:**
- Porch exhale, golden hour
- Afternoon focus flow at desk (no 2pm wall)
- Sleeping through the night (clock: 6:40 AM, she's just waking)
- Evening ritual 2.0: couch, tea/water in the old wine glass, gummy taken, exhale
- Morning coffee ENJOYED (not needed)
- Book picked back up / garden gloves back on
- Walking because she wants to (reclaims the "walks" symbol)
