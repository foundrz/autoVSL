# Airmoto — Skeptic Gift

**SCRIPT**
"So my son got me this weird smart pump as a gift. At first I thought it was kind of crazy. I mean, how could this tiny thing replace my trusty old air compressor? But after trying it just one time, I couldn't believe my eyes. It inflated my tire in minutes. It didn't stop there. Bikes, sports balls, even my motorcycle, all handled with ease. Consider me a convert. Air Moto is now my go-to and I couldn't be happier. Get yours today and see the difference for yourself."

## Why it works
The skeptic-to-convert arc pre-handles the viewer's own doubt. The gift framing ("my son got me") removes sales intent entirely — a real person was surprised, not sold.
