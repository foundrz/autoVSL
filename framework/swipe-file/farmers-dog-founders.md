# Farmer's Dog — Founders Story

**INTRODUCTION / FOUNDERS**
"Hi, I'm Brett. And I'm Jonathan. This is Darla and this is Buddy. We're the founders of the Farmer's Dog. We've made an entirely new way to feed your dog."

**THE ORIGIN STORY**
"It all started with my first dog, Jada. I got Jada in my early twenties. It was just love at first sight. She was the most adorable smart dog, but her stomach was just constantly a problem. Nothing seemed to help her until a vet recommended that I started home cooking for her. All of her issues went away. Her life changed."

**DISCOVERY OF THE PROBLEM (INDUSTRY EXPOSÉ)**
"We started looking into what is dog food? How is it made? Kibble can be made with ingredients that would be illegal to sell to a human for consumption, then turned into a powder using extreme heat, extruded into the brown balls we all know… and then sprayed with nutrients and palatants so the dogs will actually eat it. We were told kibble was the best thing you could feed your dog — premium meat for a natural diet. Turns out it wasn't. We were told all the food was real meat and vegetables. That's also not true."

**THE HUMAN HEALTH COMPARISON**
"I think we all know as human beings that eating highly processed food every single meal, every single day of our lives is not ideal for our health. But pet food being highly processed was just the status quo."

**THE DECISION TO CHANGE THE INDUSTRY**
"So we decided, let's change it... how do we make food that's fresh, healthy, and convenient, but do it for every dog across the country?"

**BRINGING IN THE EXPERT**
"We really need to get somebody who knows Dog Nutrition... Jonathan called and called and then he called again. [Expert:] 'You guys have an interesting idea — something that's revolutionary in the dog food world, and if you can pull this off, you'll have a whole new way of feeding dogs.'"

**THE PERSONALIZED SOLUTION**
"We ask a few questions about your dog, and we develop a personalized meal plan... We don't look at them as just a number. We know their name, we know their dog's name."

**HOW IT'S MADE (TRUST DETAIL)**
"We start with meat and vegetables, gently cook them and add all the nutrients dogs need... it's all human grade, which means it's made to the same safety standards as human food. Then we put it in a box, add the ice, and deliver it right to your door. It's all done for you."

**THE ADVOCACY PROMISE**
"From the moment you order your first box, you have an advocate for your dog on your side. It is not about what we do to the food. It's about what we don't do."

**GROWTH STORY + MISSION**
"What started with just Jada ended up going from one dog to eight dogs, to then a hundred dogs, and now millions... We really believe that everybody wants to take care of their dog in the best way that they can. Our mission is just to help them do that."

**CLOSING / EMOTIONAL MOMENT**
"This is the best. I can't move now."

## Why it works
The definitive founder-story structure: personal origin (love + a problem) → industry exposé ("what they don't tell you") → mission decision → expert validation → how-it's-made transparency → "what we DON'T do" reframe → growth proof → mission close. Trust compounds beat by beat.
