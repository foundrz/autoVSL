# Shot List Example (reference structure)

The gold-standard output format for Stage 5. Every generated shot list should
follow these categories and this level of detail.

---

## ✅ WHAT YOU NEED

**Props:**
- Phone (alarm, doom scrolling, group chat)
- Laptop/computer
- Gym clothes & weights
- Wedding ring (visible in shots)
- Bed/bedroom setup
- Kitchen/dinner table
- Kids toys/mess (chaos vibes)
- Book (Kyle's book)
- Journal
- Bible
- Coffee mug
- Mirror
- Scale
- Belt (gut over belt shot)
- Couch
- TV remote
- Front door

**Wardrobe:**
- Regular dad clothes (t-shirts, jeans)
- Gym clothes (before = baggy, after = fitted)
- Work clothes (if applicable)
- Shirtless for transformation shots (if comfortable)

**Tips:**
- Each clip: 3-5 seconds
- Good lighting (natural light is fine)
- Steady camera (tripod or propped phone)
- Natural acting — don't overdo it
- Film "before" and "after" versions of same scenarios
- Real emotion > perfect performance

## 🎣 HOOK SHOTS (24 total)

**Marriage/Bedroom Problems:**
- Lying in bed, wife rolls away from you (back turned)
- Reaching for her, she's already asleep
- Sitting on edge of bed alone (head in hands)
- Looking at her across the room, she doesn't notice
- Wedding ring shot — spinning it, looking at it
- Sleeping on opposite sides of bed (wide gap between you)
- Her saying goodnight without eye contact (walking away)
- Watching her get ready for bed (she doesn't look at you)

**Anger/Kids Problems:**
- Yelling at kids (mouth open, frustrated face)
- Kids flinching or looking scared
- Walking away after yelling (guilt on face)
- Head in hands after snapping
- Kids running away from you (not toward you)
- Slamming something down in frustration
- Deep breath trying to calm down (failing)
- Staring at yourself in mirror after losing it

**Body/Energy Problems:**
- Looking at gut in mirror (disappointed)
- Gut hanging over belt
- Struggling to tie shoes (out of breath)
- Stepping on scale (grimace)
- Old photo vs. now comparison (holding phone)
- Shirtless disappointment shot
- Exhausted on couch after work
- Can't keep up with kids (hands on knees)

## 😫 PROBLEM SHOTS (20 total)

**Failed Solutions:**
- Dusty gym equipment in garage/corner
- Unopened fitness programs/books on shelf
- Calendar with "START DIET" crossed out multiple times
- Gym membership card (unused)
- Fitness apps on phone (abandoned)
- Journal with only first few pages filled
- Standing on scale, no change (frustrated)
- "Day 1 again" energy (defeated)

**The Loneliness/Isolation:**
- Scrolling phone alone on couch (zoned out)
- Eating alone at counter (wife somewhere else)
- Staring at ceiling in bed (can't sleep)
- Driving alone (thousand-yard stare)
- Sitting in car before going inside (not ready to face home)
- At kids' event but mentally somewhere else
- Group of guys laughing — you're not part of it
- Church/event — putting on fake smile

**The Cycle:**
- Doom scrolling late at night (guilty face)
- Closing laptop quickly (hiding something)
- Deleting browser history
- Phone face-down on table
- Looking over shoulder nervously
- The "after everyone's asleep" moment

## 🗣️ SPOKESPERSON SHOTS (10+ angles)

Film the spokesperson talking from multiple setups:
- Straight to camera (main talking head)
- Slight angle shift (15-20 degrees left)
- Slight angle shift (15-20 degrees right)
- Sitting at desk/table
- Standing in gym
- Walking and talking
- Outside (natural light)
- Close-up (intense moments)
- Wide shot (full body)
- Holding book in hand
- Phone in hand showing group chat

**Tone variations to capture:**
- Serious/direct (calling out the problem)
- Empathetic (validating their pain)
- Fired up (the solution)
- Calm/peaceful (the transformation)
- Brother-to-brother (real talk)

## 📖 STORY SHOTS (16 total)

**The Rock Bottom:**
- "Slim Shaky" era (old photos if available)
- Sitting alone, defeated
- Knee injury moment (grabbing knees in pain)
- Bathroom floor breakdown (head down, broken)
- Looking in mirror — hating what you see
- Wife walking away (back turned)
- Lying in bed staring at ceiling

**The Discovery:**
- Finding the brotherhood / crew of men
- First workout with the guys
- Phone notification (group chat message)
- Reading/studying (learning the system)
- Alarm going off at 5am — actually getting up

**The Climb Back:**
- Gym at 5am (dark outside, lights on inside)
- Posting in group chat (accountability)
- Meal prep / tracking food
- Consistent daily routine montage
- Mirror progress — starting to see change
- Crying in mirror moment (pride, not shame)

**The Result:**
- Wife reaching for him
- Kids running TO him at the door
- Calm moment with family (peace, not chaos)
- Shirtless confidence shot
- Leading the family (prayer, dinner, activity)

## ⭐ BENEFIT SHOTS (24 total)

**Marriage Restored:**
- Wife looking at you with desire ("the look")
- Her hand on your back/arm (casual touch)
- Her initiating (reaching for you)
- Laughing together on couch
- Eye contact across the room (connection)
- Date night energy (dressed up, excited)
- Holding hands
- Her head on your shoulder
- Morning coffee together (peace)
- Her watching you (admiring)

**Kids/Family:**
- Kids running TO the door when you get home
- Calm discipline moment (patient, not angry)
- Playing with kids (present, not distracted)
- Kids looking up at you (respect)
- Teaching moment (engaged)
- Family dinner (you're leading it)
- Kids hugging you (they want to be close)
- Tucking them in (peaceful)

**Body Transformation:**
- Shirtless confidence (shoulders back)
- Belt fitting better (notches tighter)
- Lifting weights with ease
- Mirror shot — proud of reflection
- Clothes fitting better
- Energy throughout the day (no crash)
- Morning workout (crushing it)
- Veins/muscle definition (if applicable)

**Inner Peace:**
- Deep breath — calm, not stressed
- Walking through door with peace (not dread)
- Patient moment (kids are loud, you're calm)
- Journaling
- Morning quiet time (Bible, coffee, peace)
- Confident posture (head up, shoulders back)

## 👥 BROTHERHOOD SHOTS (12 total)

**The Community:**
- Phone showing group chat (messages coming in)
- Guys working out together
- Zoom call with other men (brotherhood)
- Text from brother checking in
- Posting accountability (wake time, workout)
- Guys laughing together (real friendship)
- Praying together
- Real conversation (not surface level)

**The Accountability:**
- Alarm goes off, you get up (because they're watching)
- Posting your workout
- Getting called out (in love)
- Celebrating a win (guys hyping you up)

- Book arriving (package at door)
- Unboxing the book
- Holding the book
- Book open, reading

- Highlighting/taking notes in book

**The Call:**

- Taking notes during call
- Smiling/nodding (breakthrough moment)

**The App/Program:**
- Phone showing workout plan
- Checking off daily tasks
- Watching daily video from Kyle
