# Universal Story Extraction Framework

Pull the real story behind any product experience.

**How to use:** If you have a personal story, or want to capture a customer
story, first have the AI **rewrite the following questions specifically for you
and your situation**. Then voice-record your answers — talk into the microphone
like you're talking to a friend, no filter, no script. Capture as many details
as possible. Save the transcript into `products/<slug>/stories/`, then paste it
into the session so the AI has the full story.

We want the real story, the pain, the struggle, the surprise, the emotion.

---

## 1. Introduction
- Can you introduce yourself? (Name, age, what you do, and what kind of person you are — active, entrepreneur, parent, athlete, creative, etc.)
- Before using [PRODUCT], what was your life like in the area it addresses?
- How did the problem first start showing up for you?
- What was your biggest frustration at that time?

## 2. The Nightmare (The Low Point / The Pain)
- Describe the worst moment or phase that made you realize something had to change.
- What kind of struggles, setbacks, or decline were you experiencing?
- How did it make you feel emotionally? (defeated, scared, frustrated, ashamed, stuck, etc.)
- What things did you have to give up or miss out on because of it?
- How was it affecting other parts of your life — relationships, work, confidence, daily joy?
- Did you ever think, "Maybe this is just how it is for me now"?

## 3. Failed Solutions / Frustration Phase
*(Stack tension and credibility — the things that didn't work before)*
- What did you try before [PRODUCT] that didn't work?
- How much money, time, or energy do you think you spent trying to fix it?
- What did those solutions promise you — and how did they let you down?
- What were you thinking right before you found [PRODUCT]? (Had you almost given up?)

## 4. The Discovery Moment
*(The "how I found it" story that humanizes the narrative)*
- How did you first hear about [PRODUCT]? (Friend, podcast, professional, ad, article?)
- What made you stop and pay attention?
- What made you decide to actually try it?
- What were you hoping it would do for you?
- Did you have any doubts or fears before trying it?

## 5. The First Few Days (The Experiment)
*(Sensory, emotional detail that brings the story to life)*
- What happened in the first few days or the first week?
- Did you notice anything surprising — even small?
- When did you first realize, "Wait… this is actually working"?
- What changes did you notice first?
- What did your days start to feel like compared to before?

## 6. The Transformation (The Breakthrough Phase)
*(This is your "miracle moment" — the turning point)*
- When did things really start to shift?
- How is [the problem area] now compared to before?
- How has it changed your performance — in work, daily life, hobbies, or relationships?
- What are people around you noticing? (Comments, compliments, reactions)
- Can you describe a specific day or moment that felt like, "I got my life back"?

## 7. The Emotional Turning Point
*(Pulls the heartstrings — deeper purpose, gratitude, realization)*
- What does it mean to you to feel like this again?
- How does it make you feel about your future now?
- Has it changed your outlook on what's possible for you?
- How do you feel emotionally compared to before?
- If you could describe this in one sentence — what changed most?

## 8. The Higher Calling / New Mission
*(Turning pain into purpose)*
- If you could help others who feel like you once did, what would you tell them?
- What do you wish people knew about [PRODUCT] or about solving this problem in general?
- Why do you think people overlook this solution?
- What do you feel called to do now that you've had this transformation?

## 9. Daily Life Now / Identity Reborn
*(Show them thriving — confidence, capability, new identity)*
- What's your routine like now?
- What can you do now that you couldn't before?
- How do you feel when you start each day now?
- What's one recent moment where you thought, "This is who I really am again"?

## 10. Micro-Success Timeline
*(For "Day 1 → Day 7 → Day 30" storytelling structure)*
Walk through your experience step by step:
- What did you notice after the first day or two?
- What was different by Day 3–5?
- What happened by Day 7?
- What did people start saying by Day 14 or 30?
- What surprised you most about the results?

## 11. Close with Gratitude + Call to Action
*(Emotional closure and social proof)*
- How would you describe [PRODUCT] to someone curious but skeptical?
- What would you say to your past self before starting?
- How thankful are you for discovering it?
- How would your life be different today if you had never found it?

---

## Interviewer Notes
- Replace [PRODUCT] with the specific product/service name
- Adapt "problem area" language to fit the category (health, productivity, relationships, business, etc.)
- Let them ramble — the gold is in the tangents
- Follow up on emotion: "Tell me more about how that felt"
