# Kindergarten Simple Pitch Generator

Run once per avatar. Save the output as `products/<slug>/avatars/<avatar-slug>.md`.
This file is the input to Stage 3 (Angle Generation).

---

Organize customer language into these categories. Organize everything into the
following categories so we can capture our avatar's mindset and why they'd want
to buy our product in their own words. Highlight the 80/20 Most Important ideas
for each section. I want them to feel like THAT'S ME!! Keep it extremely simple.
Use only words the average American would understand. 6.5 reading level, in a
way EVERYONE on the planet could understand what we're saying here.

[OUR PRODUCT]
{{PASTE IN INFORMATION ABOUT YOUR PRODUCT AND OPPORTUNITY}}

**AVATAR & AGE:** [Identity in their words + age range]

## THE PROBLEM (80/20 Most Important)
**Primary Problems:**
- [Use "I can't..." format with specific metrics/numbers]
- [3-5 main problems they care about MOST]

**Secondary Problems:**
- [2-3 supporting issues]

## THE EMOTIONAL PAIN
- Describe the deep emotional pain that hurts like daggers these problems are
  causing them [First-person monologue, 2-4 sentences. Capture identity crisis.]
- "I'm [doing everything right], but [betrayal]. I'm becoming [identity loss].
  [Desperate question]."

## THE ROOT CAUSE OF THE PROBLEM
- Explain what the demographic believes has been the root cause of their problems

## THE ROOT SOLUTION (Big Idea)
- Explain what they believe is the root solution to that problem along with the
  "Ah Ha" moment for them

## HOW THE PRODUCT SOLVES THIS
[Simple step-by-step flow in the avatar's language for how they believe the
product gets this result]
1. [Product does X]
2. [This triggers Y]
3. [Results in Z]

**Key Differentiator:** [Why this vs alternatives]

## THE AMAZING BENEFITS (80/20 Most Important)
**Primary Benefits:**
- [#1 benefit showing specific metrics/transformations]
- [#2 benefit]

**Secondary Benefits:**
- [Other benefits they mention]
- [Include BEFORE → AFTER contrasts where possible]

## THE EMOTIONAL BENEFITS
- Describe the deep emotional benefits that feel amazing these solutions will
  give them [Quotes + implied benefits about confidence/identity restoration]

## SIDE EFFECTS
- Most Common: [What most experience]
- 80/20 Takeaway: [Risk summary]

---

## KEY RULES
1. **Synthesize, don't list** — combine fragments into cohesive narratives
2. **Extract all numbers** — metrics make it real
3. **Use "I" statements** — first-person emotional voice
4. **Label 80/20** — what matters MOST to majority
5. **Show contrasts** — before vs after transformation

**Success test:** Could a copywriter write an ad in 5 minutes from this?
If no, you failed.
