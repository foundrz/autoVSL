# 10X-20X Market Finder Prompt (Monster Market)

Finds the avatar that represents 10–20x more people than the others.
This is live research — real threads, real language, real links.
(Future: research agent runs Phase 1 with Apify / reddit-mcp tools.)

---

PRODUCT: {{Your product name}}
MAIN BENEFIT: {{What it does}}

## PHASE 1: FIND THE AVATARS

Search for these 3 patterns:
1. "Reddit [main benefit] over [age group]"
2. "Reddit can't [desired outcome] anymore"
3. "Reddit [problem] despite trying everything"

List 5-10 threads you find and note:
- How many upvotes/comments (engagement = market size)
- What age/demographic is posting
- What language they use

## PHASE 2: IDENTIFY THE BIGGEST MARKET

Look at all threads and answer:
- Which demographic shows up in MOST threads? (age, gender, activity)
- Which problem has MOST engagement? (upvotes, comments, replies)
- Which pain is MOST emotional? (desperation, identity crisis, status loss)

The demographic + problem combo that's 10-20x bigger = YOUR PRIMARY MARKET

- AVATAR IDENTITY (2-4 words): [Example: "40+ guy who lifts"]
- CORE PROBLEM (1-2 words): [What's broken? Example: "Can't recover"]
- MOST IMPORTANT OUTCOME: [What do they want? Use their words from posts]
- CORE BENEFIT (1-2 words): [What your product delivers. Example: "Fast recovery"]
- WHO THEY SEEK ADVICE FROM: [Quote from threads — who do they trust?]
- WORLD-CLASS EXPERT: [Who would they see as the authority?]
- THE BIG IDEA: [What belief shift makes them buy? Extract from how successful users describe it]
- REDDIT SOURCE LINKS: [Paste the actual thread URLs]

- MARKET SIZE (1-10 scale): [Based on thread volume, engagement, demographic size]
- MARKET SOPHISTICATION LEVEL (Eugene Schwartz):
  - Level 1: Unaware (don't know they have the problem)
  - Level 2: Problem Aware (know problem, not solutions)
  - Level 3: Solution Aware (know solutions exist, comparing options)
  - Level 4: Product Aware (know your product, need convincing)
  - Level 5: Most Aware (ready to buy, just need offer)
