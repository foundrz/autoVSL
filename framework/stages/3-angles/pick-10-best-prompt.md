# Pick the 10 Best Ideas Prompt

Run after the Banger Angle Generator produces its full list.

---

Okay out of all of these angles, I'm looking to only pick 10 of the best — which
would those be?

Just write the headline back to me along with who should be the spokesperson and
the 3 simple bullet points for what it's about. Nothing else.

Don't write the word "spokesperson" every time. Just simplify the authority
figure's title down to 2-3 words & their age so it's easy to see. Have the title
break after the headline.

Have no more than 3-6 authority figures. Organize and chunk them down by the
authority figures and have their gender listed.
